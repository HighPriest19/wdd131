Gaming Website Plan

By Olasunkanmi Hinckley Olagunju

Site Name

Next Level Gaming Hub

This name reflects the site’s focus on gaming news, reviews, tips and culture. Optional domain: nextlevelgaminghub.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for gamers: news, upcoming releases, reviews, guides, and a gallery. The goal is help players discover games and improve their skills.

Scenarios

  • What are the most anticipated releases this year?
  • Where can I find reviews and tips for my favorite game?
